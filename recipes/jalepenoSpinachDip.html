Jalapeno Spinach Dip

This Jalapeno Spinach Dip is a creamy and spicy appetizer that's perfect for any gathering. Made with a blend of cream cheese, sour cream, and mayonnaise, it's rich and flavorful. Fresh spinach and jalapenos add a vibrant green color and a kick of heat, while shredded cheese brings everything together with a gooey, melty texture. Serve it warm with tortilla chips, crackers, or fresh vegetables for a crowd-pleasing snack that's sure to be a hit.

Whether you're hosting a party or just looking for a delicious dip to enjoy at home, this Jalapeno Spinach Dip is easy to make and always a favorite. The combination of creamy and spicy flavors makes it irresistible, and it's versatile enough to pair with a variety of dippers. Give it a try and watch it disappear in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced, white part only
  • 2 fresh jalapenos, seeded and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 (8 ounce) bag fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Greens seasoning, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan, plus more for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Heat butter or olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium heat. Add green onions, jalapenos, and garlic; cook and stir for 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Add spinach, greens seasoning, and water. Cook and stir until spinach is wilted, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir cream cheese and mayonnaise together in a separate bowl; stir in spinach and Parmesan cheese. Spread evenly into an oven safe dish. Sprinkle with a little Parmesan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, 20 to 30 minutes.
